About the Departments

Government Polytechnic Soraba is a technical institute offering three years Diploma courses. The institution is under the control of Department Of Technical Education, Government of Karnataka.


The institution was established in the year 1996, three year Diploma courses in the following branches were started.


1. DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

2. DIPLOMA IN ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

3. DIPLOMA IN CIVIL ENGINEERING

4. COMMERCIAL PRACTICE


At present, the institution is offering four Diploma programmes of three years duration and Mechanical and Automobile braches will start Shortly.
